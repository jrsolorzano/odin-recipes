Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, spices, and other fillings, and then cooked on a griddle or stove. Traditionally, a corn tortilla is used, but it can also be made with a flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on griddle
  2. Add cheese to tortilla
  3. Fold in half
  4. Wait a few minutes until cheese starts melting and tortilla starts turning brown
  5. Turn the quesadilla to the other side
  6. Wait a few minutes and done
